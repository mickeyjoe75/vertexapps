About Us

We build the data infrastructure that powers decisions

Vertex Apps is a specialist Data & Analytics consultancy with over 25 years of experience delivering and managing Business Intelligence, Data Engineering, and Full Stack Software Development projects for global clients across various industries.

Our mission is to help businesses leverage data and technology to optimise their performance, improve their customer experience, and achieve their strategic goals through leveraging AI tools.

Our core competencies include designing and developing data-driven applications using PowerBI, SSIS, Tableau, Snowflake, Google BigQuery, AWS, Postgres SQL, T-SQL, MS SQL Server, JavaScript, MongoDB, Python, Django and Ruby.
